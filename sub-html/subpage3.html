Tempura

Light, crispy Japanese-style shrimp and vegetables coated in cold batter and fried until golden. The best bite is crunchy outside and tender inside.

30 minutes 4 servings Crispy and light

Ingredients

  • 12 shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup ice-cold water
  • 1/2 cup sliced vegetables such as carrots, eggplant, or squash
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Tempura sauce or soy sauce for serving

Instructions

  1. Pat the shrimp and vegetables dry so the batter sticks well.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat.
  3. In a bowl, lightly mix egg and ice-cold water.
  4. Add flour and stir gently. Keep the batter a little lumpy for a crisp texture.
  5. Dip shrimp and vegetables into the batter.
  6. Fry in small batches until light golden and crisp.
  7. Drain on a rack or paper towel, then serve immediately with sauce.
