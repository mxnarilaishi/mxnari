Bopis

A bold Filipino dish made with finely chopped pork lungs and heart, cooked with garlic, onion, vinegar, and chilies for a spicy tangy bite.

55 minutes 4 servings Spicy and tangy

Ingredients

  • 500g pork lungs and heart, boiled and finely chopped
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 red chilies, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, then saute garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped pork lungs and heart, then cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in vinegar and let it boil for a few minutes without stirring.
  4. Add soy sauce, bell pepper, and chilies, then mix well.
  5. Simmer until the liquid reduces and the flavor becomes rich.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

Why I love it

Big flavor in every bite

Bopis stands out because it is savory, spicy, and tangy. It is the kind of dish that makes plain rice exciting.
