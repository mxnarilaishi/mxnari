Sinigang Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g shrimp
  • 1 packet sinigang mix
  • 2 tomatoes quartered
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1 radish (labanos) sliced
  • 2 green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 6 cups water
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare Ingredients:Clean and devein shrimp, slice radish, tomatoes
    wash kangkong leaves
  2. Boil base:In a pot, add water and tomatoes. Let it boil until tomatoes
    soften.
  3. Add sour flavor:Mix in sinigang mix for that signature tangy taste.
  4. Add radish:Drop in radish slices and cook until tender.
  5. Add shrimp:Place the shrimp into the pot and simmer for 3-5 minutes
    until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  6. Season:Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Finish with greens:Add kangkong leaves last, simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Serve hot:Enjoy with steamed rice.

This is my top 1 in the list and I love it because of how sour and tasty it is combined.
