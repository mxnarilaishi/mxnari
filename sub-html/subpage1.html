Sinigang Shrimp

A warm Filipino sour soup with juicy shrimp, tamarind broth, tomatoes, radish, and leafy greens. It is bright, comforting, and perfect with rice.

35 minutes 4 servings Sour and savory

Ingredients

  • 500g shrimp, cleaned and deveined
  • 1 packet sinigang mix or tamarind soup base
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 2 green chili peppers
  • 1 bunch kangkong leaves
  • 6 cups water
  • Fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the water with tomatoes and onion until the tomatoes soften.
  2. Stir in the sinigang mix and let the broth become tangy.
  3. Add the radish slices and simmer until tender.
  4. Add the shrimp and cook for 3 to 5 minutes, until pink and firm.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add kangkong and green chilies, then simmer for 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Why I love it

Sour, cozy, and always satisfying

This is my top 1 because the sour broth and seafood flavor taste fresh and comforting at the same time.
