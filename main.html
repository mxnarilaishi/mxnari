Featured Foods

Top 1

Sinigang Shrimp

Shrimp sinigang is a classic Filipino sour soup made with fresh shrimp simmered in a tangy broth flavored by tamarind, tomatoes, and onions. It is often cooked with kangkong, radish, okra, and eggplant for a comforting balance of savory seafood and refreshing sourness.

Top 2

Bopis

Bopis is a traditional Filipino dish made from finely chopped pork lungs and heart, sauteed with garlic, onions, vinegar, and chilies. It is known for its bold, tangy, and spicy flavor, often enjoyed with rice or served as pulutan.

Top 3

Tempura

Tempura is a popular Japanese dish made by coating seafood or vegetables in a light batter and frying them until crisp and golden. The delicate crunch highlights the natural flavor of the ingredients and pairs well with tentsuyu sauce or salt.
